Who We Are

A Movement for National Transformation

Re-Orientation Advocacy of Nigeria (RAN), also known as Re-Orientation Advocacy Foundation of Nigeria (CAC/IT/NO.66122), is a non-governmental organization committed to guiding Nigerian youth back to core values and principles that foster responsible citizenship and national development.

Nigeria faces growing challenges including corruption, insecurity, abuse of power, drug abuse, kidnapping, and declining moral standards. RAN exists to confront these issues through structured re-orientation, civic education, and youth-focused advocacy.
